Privacy Policy

WhatsApp Status Saver App

Important Notice: This app is designed to help you save WhatsApp statuses from your own device. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data remains secure.

1. Information We Collect

Our WhatsApp Status Saver app is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best possible experience:

Data We Access:

  • Local Storage Access: The app accesses your device's local storage to locate WhatsApp status files
  • Media Files: We access status images and videos stored locally on your device
  • App Usage Data: Basic usage statistics to improve app performance (optional)

Data We Do NOT Collect:

  • Personal messages or chat content
  • Contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking
  • Any data that could identify you personally

2. How We Use Your Information

The information accessed by our app is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To display available WhatsApp statuses for saving
  • To enable you to save status media to your device
  • To organize and manage saved content within the app
  • To improve app functionality and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • Local Storage: All status files are processed and stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not upload your media files to any external servers
  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted when stored locally
  • Secure Access: We use secure methods to access WhatsApp status folders

4. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics: To understand app usage patterns (anonymized data only)
  • Crash Reporting: To identify and fix app issues
  • Advertisements: To support free app development (if applicable)

These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them.

5. Permissions

Our app requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Storage Access: To read and save WhatsApp status files
  • Media Access: To display and organize media files
  • Internet Access: For optional features like updates and analytics

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access and view what data the app processes
  • Delete saved files at any time
  • Disable analytics and optional data sharing
  • Uninstall the app to remove all associated data

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently received personal information from a child under 13, we will delete such information from our records.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy within the app. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.
